Tyler Barrett's CSCE 190 Portfolio

Problem Statement: Candle Customization

The users are frustrated because of not finding the perfect scent, shape, and style for their specific needs. People are tired of going to the stores especially during this pandemic. As a result, our solution provides users a way to easily create the perfect candle for them and risk free during the pandemic.
